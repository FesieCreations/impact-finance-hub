About Impact Finance Hub

Impact Finance Hub is a student-led initiative at the University of Malawi (UNIMA) dedicated to empowering young people with practical financial literacy, investment awareness, and entrepreneurial skills.

Our Vision

To create a generation of financially confident, innovative, and economically empowered young Malawians who actively contribute to their personal growth, their communities, and the national economy. We envision students equipped not just with knowledge, but with practical skills to make smart financial decisions, explore investment opportunities, and embrace entrepreneurship responsibly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between financial theory and real-life application by providing accessible, practical, and student-driven financial education. We aim to nurture confident students who can manage their personal finances effectively, understand local and global financial opportunities, and take bold steps toward creating economic self-sufficiency and positive impact in their communities.

Why Impact Finance Hub Exists

Many students graduate with strong academic knowledge but limited practical financial skills. Impact Finance Hub was created to help students confidently manage money, understand opportunities, make informed financial decisions, and unlock their full potential as future leaders, entrepreneurs, and financially independent individuals.

Our Core Values

  • Accessibility: Finance education for all students
  • Practicality: Learning that applies to real life
  • Integrity: Ethical and responsible financial thinking
  • Impact: Knowledge that creates meaningful change
