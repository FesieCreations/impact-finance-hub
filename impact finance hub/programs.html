Programs & Activities

Impact Finance Hub runs practical, student-focused programs designed to bridge the gap between financial theory and real-world application. Our activities empower students with skills they can use immediately.

Our Core Programs

Financial Literacy Workshops

Interactive sessions focused on budgeting, saving, managing expenses, and building healthy financial habits as a student.

Investment Awareness Sessions

Educational talks that introduce students to investment concepts, risk management, and long-term financial planning.

Entrepreneurship Training

Practical training designed to help students identify opportunities, start small ventures, and develop basic business skills.

Talks, Panels & Guest Speakers

Discussions with professionals, entrepreneurs, and experienced individuals sharing real-world insights and lessons.

Peer Learning & Mentorship

Student-led discussions and mentorship sessions that encourage collaboration, accountability, and shared growth.

How Students Participate

Attend Programs

Students can participate in workshops, training sessions, and talks organized by the hub throughout the academic year.

Become a Member

Membership provides access to exclusive sessions, peer learning, and opportunities to grow within the hub.

Volunteer & Contribute

Members can volunteer in organizing events, content creation, and community outreach activities.

Leadership Opportunities

Students can develop leadership skills by serving in committees or executive roles within the hub.

Get Involved Today

Do not wait to start building your financial knowledge and entrepreneurial skills. Join the Impact Finance Hub to attend programs, workshops, and hands-on activities designed for students like you.
